Experimenting with feature scaling. Spent three hours wondering why a dummy gradient descent script I wrote wasn't converging. It turned out my features were on completely different magnitudes—one column ranged from 0 to 1, while another went up to 50,000.

Notes: Applied `StandardScaler` to normalize everything to zero mean and unit variance. The convergence curve flattened out beautifully. It's wild how a simple mathematical alignment can make or break an optimization step. Belief strengthening: raw data is an absolute lie until you structure it.

Crisp and clear. Felt a bit blocked, but clean code saved the day. Continued working on my learning goals.
