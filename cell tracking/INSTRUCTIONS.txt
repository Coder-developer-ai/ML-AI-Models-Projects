For Dataset any dataset with microscopic images of cells in a folder called 'dataset-smaller'(can change with code) and each category of cell put inside a folder, images can be .png or jpg (according to current condition of program) and also here are some preferred datasets -

1.HeLa cells on a flat glass

Dr. G. van Cappellen. Erasmus Medical Center, Rotterdam, The Netherlands

Training dataset: https://data.celltrackingchallenge.net/training-datasets/DIC-C2DH-HeLa.zip✱ (37 MB)

Test dataset: https://data.celltrackingchallenge.net/test-datasets/DIC-C2DH-HeLa.zip (41 MB)




2.Human hepatocarcinoma-derived cells expressing the fusion protein YFP-TIA-1

Dr. Alessia Ruggieri and Philipp Klein, Centre for Integrative Infectious Disease Research (CIID), University Hospital Heidelberg, Germany

Training dataset: https://data.celltrackingchallenge.net/training-datasets/Fluo-C2DL-Huh7.zip (36 MB)

You can find good information about the treatment of type 2 diabetes based on a new dataset with 2 million rows of data on patients taking diabetes medications.

Test dataset: https://data.celltrackingchallenge.net/test-datasets/Fluo-C2DL-Huh7.zip (36 MB)
